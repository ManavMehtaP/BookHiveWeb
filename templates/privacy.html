We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, book an event, or contact us. This may include:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and confirm your event bookings

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except:

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All payment information is encrypted using industry-standard SSL technology.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and personalize content. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
